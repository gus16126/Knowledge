**The Importance of Effective Communication and Professional Vocabulary in Leadership**

Effective communication is a fundamental leadership skill and an essential component of a successful workplace. At CarMax, communication goes beyond simply exchanging information—it is about building trust, creating alignment, and fostering a culture where associates feel valued and supported.

When communicating with managers and leadership, using clear, professional, and respectful language helps ensure that messages are understood accurately and that important information is not lost or misunderstood. Professional vocabulary demonstrates preparation, confidence, and a commitment to constructive dialogue. It allows associates to communicate concerns, share ideas, and discuss opportunities for improvement in a way that contributes positively to the team and the business.

Strong communication directly supports CarMax's value of **Win Together** by encouraging collaboration, transparency, and teamwork. When associates and leaders communicate openly and effectively, they are better able to solve problems, achieve goals, and support one another in delivering excellent results.

Communication also reflects **Put People First**. Listening actively, speaking respectfully, and choosing appropriate language shows consideration for others and helps create an environment where everyone feels heard and respected. This strengthens relationships between associates and management and promotes a positive workplace culture.

In addition, effective communication supports **Go for Greatness** by allowing teams to share knowledge, identify opportunities, and continuously improve processes. Leaders rely on accurate and timely communication to make informed decisions that benefit both associates and customers.

Finally, professional communication demonstrates **Do the Right Thing** by promoting honesty, accountability, and integrity. Whether discussing safety concerns, operational challenges, or performance goals, communicating clearly and respectfully helps ensure that decisions are based on facts and that everyone remains focused on achieving the best outcomes.

As associates develop their leadership skills, mastering effective communication and professional vocabulary becomes increasingly important. These skills help build credibility, strengthen professional relationships, and contribute to the success of both the individual and Team CarMax.
