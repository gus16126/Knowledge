**The Purpose of Recognition at CarMax**

Recognition is more than a thank you—it is a way to reinforce the behaviors, values, and actions that help our teams succeed together.

When we recognize a teammate, we acknowledge the positive impact of their efforts and highlight how their actions support our CarMax values: **Win Together, Put People First, Go for Greatness, and Do the Right Thing**. Recognition helps associates understand that their contributions matter and that their commitment to safety, customer experience, teamwork, learning, and operational excellence is valued.

Recognition also serves as a powerful learning tool. By highlighting specific actions and behaviors, we create examples for others to follow and help build a culture where excellence is shared, encouraged, and repeated. Whether it is supporting a teammate, following a process correctly, improving through coaching, taking initiative, or delivering an iconic customer experience, recognition helps reinforce what success looks like.

Most importantly, recognition strengthens relationships across teams and shifts. It creates an environment where people feel appreciated, supported, and motivated to continue growing. By celebrating individual and team achievements, we help build a culture of trust, accountability, continuous improvement, and shared success.

Recognition is one of the ways we invest in our people, encourage greatness, and ensure that every associate knows their efforts make a difference.
Gustavo Guallar
