📋 **Team Training & Safety – Weigh Station Awareness**  
October 6, 2025

Team,

As we expand our routes to other Florida cities like **Orlando, Lakeland, and Tampa**, you’ll start seeing **weigh stations** along major highways. These are **DOT checkpoints** used to verify vehicle weight, safety compliance, and ELD records.

If a weigh station light is **flashing or open**, **you are required to enter** unless clearly instructed otherwise. **Skipping an open weigh station can lead to enforcement action — you may be stopped by Florida Highway Patrol, cited, and fined.** Repeated or serious violations can result in out-of-service orders or additional penalties.

We’ll cover **what to expect** and **how to prepare** in the next training message, including inspection steps and required documentation.

Let’s continue to **Do the Right Thing** by staying compliant, informed, and professional on every route.
Gustavo Guallar
