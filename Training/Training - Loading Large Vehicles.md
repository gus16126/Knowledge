**Team Training Tip – Loading Large Vehicles on Two-Car Haulers**

Team,

When transporting **large vehicles such as full-size pickup trucks or similarly sized units**, the load should normally be treated as a **single-vehicle transport** due to weight and space considerations.

Recommended configuration:

• **Load the vehicle front first**.  
• Position the **front wheels on the upper ramp**, **without passing the grab pole** at the front of the carrier.  
• Keep the **upper ramp in the lower position** during loading.

After positioning the vehicle:

• **Secure the rear tires with straps.**  
• Once the vehicle is secured, **raise the upper ramp and pin it at the 1½ hole position** so the **weight of the vehicle rests on the pins** rather than on the hydraulic system.

This configuration helps maintain **proper weight distribution, equipment protection, and safe transport conditions** when handling heavier vehicles.

As always, if you have questions about setup or positioning, please reach out so we can review it together.
