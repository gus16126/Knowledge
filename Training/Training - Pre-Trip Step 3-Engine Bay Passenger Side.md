This document, provided by Gustavo Guallar, a CarMax Fleet driver, is a specialized pre-trip inspection guide of the engine bay passenger side for vehicle professionals. It ensures compliance with D.O.T. regulations and supports equipment safety and reliability. The checklist outlines a systematic inspection of the passenger side engine bay, proceeding from top to bottom and left to right. Key areas include the cooling system, electrical components such as the alternator, and the condition of belts and hoses. The guide highlights the importance of the intercooler system, noting that damaged boots can significantly reduce engine performance. By following these steps, drivers can detect potential leaks or hardware failures before they become serious issues. This thorough approach promotes safety and consistency in every vehicle assessment.
