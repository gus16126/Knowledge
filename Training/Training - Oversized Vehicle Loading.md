## **Oversized Vehicle Loading: Split-Deck Protocol**

### **1. Positioning & Boundary**

- **Split the Load:** Span the vehicle across the upper and lower decks as shown.
- **The "Grab Bar" Rule:** **DO NOT PASS THE GRAB BAR.** Ensure the front of the vehicle maintains a safe clearance from the safety rail to avoid cab damage and ensure proper weight distribution.
### **2. Initial Securement**

- **Rear Tires First:** Strap the rear tires completely before adjusting deck height. This anchors the vehicle and prevents shifting.
### **3. Deck Adjustment**

- **Pinning:** Raise the upper deck and pin it at the **1 ½ hole**.
- **Why:** This specific height is required to prevent structural stress or bending of the upper ramp extension.
### **4. Final Tie-Down**

- **Front Wheels:** Secure the front wheels only after the upper deck is pinned and locked.
