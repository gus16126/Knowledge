This is Part 3 of a 5-part training series covering key safety and operational behaviors that may be observed during a manager ride-along.

	June 14, 2026

✅ Intersections
• Approaches intersections with caution
• Stops clear of crosswalks
• Makes complete stops at stop signs and red lights

✅ Speed Management
• Smooth acceleration
• Maintains appropriate speed for conditions
• Adjusts speed for traffic, weather, and roadway conditions

✅ Braking and Vehicle Control
• Smooth brake application
• Avoids unnecessary brake use
• Tests brakes when appropriate
• Uses vehicle momentum and engine braking effectively
• Understands proper downshifting techniques
• Proper use of engine brake systems

Remember: Ride-along are opportunities to demonstrate the safe habits and professional standards we practice every day. Consistency in these areas helps protect our team, our customers, and the vehicles entrusted to us.
