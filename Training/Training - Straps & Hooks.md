🚛 **Pre-Trip Inspection Training – Straps & Hooks Matter**

Team,

During a **pre-trip inspection on Truck 142415**, I identified a safety issue related to the **condition of the straps and hooks**:

📸 In the attached photos, you’ll see:

- One hook **missing the retaining nut**
- Another hook with the **nut loose**

This was caught during the pre-trip inspection, which specifically requires us to check the **condition and security of straps, hooks, and hardware** before operating the truck.

🔍 **Why This Matters:**

- A missing or loose nut can allow the hook to fail under load.
- This creates a serious risk to vehicle securement, equipment, and safety on the road.
- These issues may not be obvious unless we take the time to closely inspect each component.

🛠️ **Reminder:**  
When performing pre-trip inspections, always take a moment to visually and physically check straps, hooks, and fasteners for damage, looseness, or missing hardware.

This is a great example of how a thorough pre-trip inspection helps us catch problems early and prevent bigger issues down the road.
