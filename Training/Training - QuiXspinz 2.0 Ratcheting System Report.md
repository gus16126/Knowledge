Report: Safe and Effective Use of the Cottrell Two-Car Truck 
and QuiXspinz 2.0 Ratcheting System 
 
Introduction 
The Cottrell QuiXspinz Ratcheting System is an innovative and efficient solution 
designed to secure vehicles on car haulers. Developed by Cottrell, a leading name in car 
hauler manufacturing, this system aims to streamline the vehicle tie-down process, 
enhancing both efficiency and safety. 
 
Overview of the QuiXspinz System 
The original Cottrell QuiXspinz system was introduced as an upgrade option on 
Cottrell's NeXtgen soft tie equipment. The system allows operators to insert the tie-down 
bar only once when fastening the strap to the tire, reducing the time required for vehicle 
securement. The enhanced QuiXspinz 2.0 version has further improved efficiency by 
offering smoother operation and better ergonomics for varied weather conditions. Refer 
to Figure 1 for a visual demonstration. 
 
How the Straps are Used 
• 
Preparation: Position the vehicle on the trailer, ensuring proper alignment.  
• 
Straps: Inspect the straps for wear and damage. Each strap has two 2" Swivel J-
Hook   
• 
Attaching the Strap: Place the strap over the tire and connect the two 2" Swivel 
J-Hooks to the truck's anchor points.   
• 
2" Swivel J-Hook: Each 2" Swivel J-Hook, as recommended by Cottrell, should 
be secured in the anchor point closest to the tire. This positioning ensures the J-
Hook serves as a wheel chock, effectively preventing vehicle movement. Refer to 
Figure 2 for a visual demonstration. 
• 
Threading the Strap into the Ratchet: Feed the free end of the strap through 
the slot in the ratchet's rotating spool. Pull the strap through it and leave some 
slack. 
• 
Ratchet Spool. As recommended by Cottrell, align the ratchet spool directly with 
the tire and ensure that the spooled strap remains fully within it. 
• 
Ratchet Shaft. The ratchet shaft allows the ratchet spool to move side-to-side, aligning 
directly with the strapped tire. If the strap crosses over the spool into the ratchet shaft, it can 
damage both the shaft and the strap, compromising the safety and functionality originally 
intended. See Figures 3, 4, and 5 for a visual reference. 


 
 
• 
Tightening the Strap: Utilize the tie-down bar on the QuiXspinz ratchet and 
engage the ratcheting mechanism to securely fasten the strap, ensuring it is not 
over-tightened. 
• 
Securing Excess Strap: Secure any excess strap to prevent it from flapping 
during transit. 
• 
Final Inspection: Ensure the strap is properly positioned and the vehicle is 
stable. 
 
Maintenance Recommendations 
• 
Regularly inspect the QuiXspinz system for wear or damage. 
• 
Keep the ratchet mechanism clean and lubricated for smooth operation. 
Conclusion 
The Cottrell QuiXspinz 2.0 Ratcheting System has proven reliable and effective for 
securing vehicles on car haulers. It offers speed, efficiency, and enhanced safety. It is 
recommended for use on various Cottrell trailers, including the CX-5308LS NextGen 
Quick Loader and the CX-09 LSFA 80-ft. trailer. 
To fully utilize the safety features of the QuiXspinz ratchet systems, ensure all 
procedures are followed as recommended. Refer to the examples in Figures 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 
and 6 to understand the consequences of incorrect usage and the proper procedures.
