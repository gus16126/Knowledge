* Why It's Important to Complete a Pre/Post-Trip Inspection with Each Use?

The Pre/Post-Trip Inspection is a critical safety procedure that must be
completed every time a vehicle is used. 

This process has been established to standardize how inspections are performed across the team, ensuring
that every driver is aligned with CarMax's expectations for safety, readiness,
operational consistency, and DOT compliance.

Completing this inspection helps ensure:
Standardization: Everyone follows the same method, reducing errors and
building consistency across all shifts and drivers.

Vehicle Safety: Mechanical issues, fluid leaks, or equipment defects can be
identified before they cause breakdowns or safety hazards.

Vehicle Readiness: Ensures the vehicle is properly equipped and prepared
for use, reducing downtime and surprises on the road.

Efficiency: Addressing issues before departure prevents delays, rework, and
potential incidents during delivery operations.

As a team, it's essential that we not only perform these inspections but also
understand why they matter. This reinforces our commitment to safety,
professionalism, and Doing the Right Thing every day.
