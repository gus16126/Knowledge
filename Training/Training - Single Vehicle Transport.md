**Team Training Tip – Single Vehicle Transport on Two-Car Hauler**

Team,

When transporting **a single sedan-size vehicle or mid-size SUV** on a two-car hauler, the recommended configuration from **Cottrell** is to **load the vehicle front first on the upper deck while keeping the upper deck in the lower position**.

As illustrated in the reference image, positioning the vehicle this way helps improve **weight distribution and overall stability of the carrier during transport**. Keeping the upper deck in the lower position also helps maintain a **lower center of gravity**, which contributes to safer handling of the truck while driving.

Whenever operating with a **single vehicle load**, consider this configuration when conditions allow, as it supports both **equipment stability and safe transport practices**.

If anyone has questions or would like to review the setup together during loading, please feel free to reach out.

Thanks for the teamwork and commitment to safe operations.
