🚛 **Purpose Of The Daily Message Training Program**

Team,

The purpose of this training program is to strengthen our knowledge, consistency, and confidence when performing our duties on our 2-Car Haulers.

Over the coming weeks, I will be sharing training messages, labeled diagrams, real-world examples, inspection techniques, and safety reminders designed to help us better understand the components we inspect every day and the reasons behind each inspection step.

This program is intended to support standardization across the team, improve mechanical awareness, identify potential issues before they become breakdowns, and reinforce our commitment to safety and operational excellence.

A thorough Pre-Trip Inspection is more than a requirement—it is one of the most effective tools we have to protect ourselves, our equipment, our customers' vehicles, and our fellow associates.

Thank you for your commitment to safety, professionalism, and continuous learning. Let's continue working together to build our knowledge and perform every inspection to the highest standard.
Gustavo Guallar
