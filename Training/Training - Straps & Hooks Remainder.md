🚛 **Pre-Trip Reminder – Straps & Hooks**

Team,

During a pre-trip inspection on **Truck 142415**, I found one strap hook **missing a nut** and another with the **nut loose** (see photos).

Pre-trip inspections require us to check the **condition and security of straps, hooks, and hardware**. A loose or missing nut can lead to securement failure under load.

Please take a moment during pre-trips to visually and physically check all straps and hooks. Catching issues early helps prevent breakdowns and safety risks.

Thanks for the support and for Doing the Right Thing.
Gustavo Guallar
