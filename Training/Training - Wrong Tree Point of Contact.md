**Safety Observation:** The associate is descending from the vehicle while holding the exterior of the vehicle with the door closed, creating an unstable position and eliminating proper three points of contact. This increases the risk of slips, falls, and injury.

**Expected Practice:** Keep the door open and use secure interior handholds such as the steering wheel, B-pillar, or door opening frame while using the deck steps. Maintaining three points of contact throughout the exit process provides maximum stability and supports our commitment to safe operating practices.
