🟦 Team Training – What Managers May Be Observing During Ride-Along

	June 12, 2026

Team as part of CarMax commitment to safety, professionalism, and operational excellence, managers conducting ride-along may silently evaluate many of the same skills included in the Road Test.

While the purpose of a ride-along is coaching and development, the following behaviors are often observed to ensure we are operating safely and representing CarMax professionally.

Part 1 of 5:

✅ Before Moving the Vehicle
• Proper operation of the ELD
• ELD mounted and clearly visible
• Pre-Trip Inspection completed

✅ Emergency Equipment
• Fire extinguisher present and accessible
• Spare fuses available
• Warning triangles/emergency devices available

Remember: Ride-along are opportunities to demonstrate the safe habits and professional standards we practice every day. Consistency in these areas helps protect our team, our customers, and the vehicles entrusted to us.
