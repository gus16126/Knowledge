![📋](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/1f4cb/32.png) **MaxOffers Pickup & Delivery Procedure**

Team,

To ensure accurate vehicle handling and proper documentation, please follow the process below for every MaxOffers pickup and delivery.

### Pickup Location

![✅](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/2705/32.png) Obtain the **Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)** from the pickup location.

![✅](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/2705/32.png) If requested, provide a **screenshot of the dispatch sheet**.

![✅](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/2705/32.png) The pickup location will provide the **vehicle key** and vehicle location.

![⚠️](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/26a0_fe0f/32.png) Pickup locations will **not** provide the title or any vehicle paperwork.

### Vehicle Verification Process

1. Take a screenshot of the dispatch sheet.
    
2. Take a clear photo of the vehicle's VIN sticker.
    
3. Compare the VIN on the dispatch sheet to the VIN sticker on the vehicle.
    
4. Verify that both VINs match **before loading the vehicle**.
    

### Documentation

Please retain both the dispatch sheet screenshot and VIN sticker photo for your records. These documents verify that the correct vehicle was loaded and can be referenced if questions arise later.

### Delivery Location

1. Bring the vehicle inside and park it in the designated **MaxOffer lot location** for that store.
    
2. Place a **Key Lockbox (Blue Box)** on the vehicle window.
    
3. Place the vehicle key inside the **Key Lockbox (Blue Box)**.
    
4. Mark the vehicle as **Delivered** in the driver application.
    

### Important

![✅](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/2705/32.png) Once the vehicle is parked, secured, key placed in the lockbox, and the delivery is completed in the driver application, **no additional paperwork or actions are required for MaxOffers vehicles.**
