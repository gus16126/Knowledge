🟦 Peer Training – Von Frazier

Training Period: September 8 – October 3, 2025

Von Frazier was trained using the refined home delivery onboarding and equipment training model. Following the successful training of Carlos Barrios, Von was onboarded with a focus on establishing strong safety habits, vehicle inspection consistency, and proper use of the 2-car hauler equipment.

Training Model Consistency with Improvements

Von received structured hands-on instruction using our standardized training methods. This included:
- Visual aid diagrams for engine component and inner-wheel inspection.
- Repetitive practice sessions for vehicle loading, deck securement, and hydraulic safety.
- Emphasis on daily Pre-Trip and Post-Trip inspections as the foundation of shift safety.
- DOT compliance, BOL documentation, and mobile tool usage (CarMax Driver App and My20 ELD).
- **ELDT Curriculum Alignment:** Although conducted without a formal classroom curriculum, my peer-training model was designed to mirror the structural requirements of the Federal FMCSA Class B CDL Entry-Level Driver Training (ELDT) curriculum. With the exception of select tractor-trailer components (such as trailer coupling/uncoupling) which do not apply to straight trucks, my hands-on instruction systematically covered all key ELDT areas: basic operation controls, safe driving procedures, visual search habits, space/space management, and range-backing maneuvers (including straight line, alley dock, and parallel docking). Aligning peer training with these federal standards ensures our home delivery drivers operate with commercial-grade safety and compliance.

Key Training Focus Areas

- **Pre-Trip Inspection & Component Diagnosis (ELDT B1.1.3 / B1.4.1):** Establishing a thorough, consistent inspection routine. Utilized custom labeled engine-bay and inner-wheel diagrams to train Von on mechanical identification and wear-and-tear diagnosis.
- **QuiXspinz Strap Securement:** Focusing on correct strap alignment and tensioning to prevent equipment wear.
- **Hydraulic Safety:** Deck pinning procedures and ramp safety during loading operations.
- **Visual Search & Space Management (ELDT B1.2.1 / B1.2.5):** Coached Von on defensive driving, mirror checks, following distance calibration for air brake lag, and tail swing awareness in dealership/customer delivery zones.
- **Range-Backing & Docking (ELDT B2.2 / B2.3):** Practiced backing alignments, setups, and dock maneuvering in controlled environments to build confidence before field routes.
- **Customer Experience:** Maintaining high standards of professionalism and communication during deliveries.

✅ Performance & Engagement

Von demonstrated exceptional discipline and safety compliance throughout his training. He took ownership of the inspection procedures immediately and showed great mechanical awareness. Since transitioning to independent routes and being assigned his own truck, Von has maintained perfect consistency, performing his Pre-Trip inspections diligently at the start of every shift.
