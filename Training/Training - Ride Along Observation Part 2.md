This is Part 2 of a 5-part training series covering key safety and operational behaviors that may be observed during a manager ride-along.

	June 13, 2026

✅ Safe Driving Habits
• Seat belt worn properly
• Proper seating position
• Limits distractions while driving
• Courteous and patient driving behavior
• Compliance with traffic signs and regulations

✅ Commercial Vehicle Operation
• Proper vehicle start-up procedures
• Monitors gauges and warning indicators
• Maintains both hands on the steering wheel when appropriate
• Maintains safe lane position
• Consistently checks mirrors
• Constant awareness of surroundings
• Maintains proper following distance

Remember: Ride-along are opportunities to demonstrate the safe habits and professional standards we practice every day. Consistency in these areas helps protect our team, our customers, and the vehicles entrusted to us.
