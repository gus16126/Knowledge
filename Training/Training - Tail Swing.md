📋 Team Training & Safety – Tail Swing Awareness
September 25, 2025

Team,

This Monday I experienced a real-life reminder of the importance of tail swing. While driving to pick up my granddaughter, I crossed an intersection at the same time a car hauler was making a right turn. As the truck swung, the tail crossed into my lane, forcing me to take serious evasive action. Unfortunately, the driver behind me couldn’t react in time and suffered major vehicle damage, including a lost front wheel.

🚛 Key Reminder:

Always be aware of where your truck’s tail is during turns.
Set up your turns appropriately and use your mirrors to ensure the tail of your truck stays inside your lane.
Proper setup and awareness can prevent costly accidents and protect others on the road.
Let’s remain mindful of this every time we operate our equipment—our awareness not only protects us but also the drivers around us.                                                                                           Gustavo Guallar
