🟦 Team Training – The Importance of the Air Dryer

June 15, 2026

The air dryer is a critical component of a commercial vehicle's air brake system. Located between the air compressor and the air reservoirs, it removes moisture, oil, and other contaminants from the compressed air before they enter the brake system.

The air dryer uses a desiccant filtration system to absorb moisture from the compressed air. This is important because moisture can cause corrosion, freezing, and premature wear of brake system components, potentially affecting brake performance and reliability.

A properly functioning air dryer helps protect the air brake system, extends component life, and contributes to the safe operation of the vehicle.

During your pre-trip inspection, pay attention to any signs of air system issues and report concerns promptly to help keep our equipment safe and road-ready.
