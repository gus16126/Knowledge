📋 **Team Training & Safety – What to Do When Entering a Weigh Station**  
October 6, 2025

![[Pasted image 20260601223822.png]]

Team — quick steps when you see a weigh station light or sign:

1. **Enter when directed.** If the station is open or the light is flashing, pull in — do not bypass. Skipping an open station is a violation and may lead to enforcement.
2. **Park where instructed and secure the truck.** Set the park brake, follow staff directions, and remain with your vehicle unless told otherwise.
3. **Have these documents ready:** CDL, Medical Examiner’s Certificate, vehicle registration/cab card, proof of insurance, ELD logs (or RODS), periodic inspection/maintenance records, and bill of lading/shipper papers. Inspectors routinely request these.
4. **Know what to expect.** Most stops are a quick weight check; some will include a safety inspection (Level II/III) where brakes, lights, securement and cargo may be checked. Be cooperative and concise with answers.
5. **If overweight or non-compliant:** you can receive a citation, fines per Florida law (penalty rate applies by pound over the limit), or an out-of-service order for serious violations. Notify dispatch and management immediately.

Short, calm, and cooperative behavior speeds the stop and protects you and CarMax. We’ll follow up with a step-by-step checklist (documents to keep in the cab, ELD checks, and how to communicate with inspectors) in the next training message.

Do the Right Thing — stay safe and professional out there.
