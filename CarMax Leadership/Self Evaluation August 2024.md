CarMax Department Specific Self Evaluation for August 2024.

August 2024.

During the CarMax fleet restructuring, my position as a Rollback driver was eliminated, and I transitioned to a role as a home delivery driver.

During my training, I became aware of the challenges associated with picking up our Max Offer vehicles from Brickell Mazda, and I wanted to find an efficient solution to address this issue.

My first attempt to collect Max Offers at Brickell Mazda was unsuccessful. It took half a day, and the vehicle was not found at the Mazda store, their external lot, or the third-party dealership they directed me to.

After half a day of searching, I discovered the vehicle was, in fact, at the third-party dealership, but I needed a release from Brickell Mazda. After securing the release from Chris Chávez, the manager, I was able to pick up the vehicle. 

Though discouraging, each setback helped me learn the vehicle release procedures at Brickell Mazda and connect with the involved individuals; within just a few visits, I had the contact information for everyone involved, was able to locate vehicles independently, gain access to vehicle keys, pick up vehicles after hours, and secure the vehicle’s release without bothering the manager.

As a result, by taking the time to learn their procedures and establish relationships with managers and their employees, I converted a complicated and challenging account that most drivers in our team avoided into my most manageable and easy account.
Gustavo Guallar
