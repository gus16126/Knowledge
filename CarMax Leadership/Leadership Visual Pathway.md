# **CarMax Leadership Pathway — One-Page Visual Guide**

## **From Readiness to Promotion**

### **Clear · Consistent · Values-Aligned**

---

## **Leadership at CarMax (Top Line)**

Leadership is not a title.  
Leadership is **consistent behavior aligned to CarMax Values**.

- **Win Together**
- **Put People First**
- **Go for Greatness**
- **Do the Right Thing**

Promotion happens when leadership is **demonstrated, developed, validated, and confirmed**.

---

## **The CarMax Leadership Pathway (Visual Flow)**

```
Leadership Behaviors        ↓MAP (Assessment)        ↓IDP (Development)        ↓LDP (Execution)        ↓Promotion Readiness
```

Each step builds on the previous one.  
No step is skipped.

---

## **MAP — Management Assessment Program**

### _How readiness is evaluated_

**What MAP Looks For**

- People leadership & coaching
- Ownership & accountability
- Decision-making & judgment
- Communication & influence
- Operational & safety leadership
- Values-based leadership

**MAP Answers One Question**

> “Is next-level leadership being demonstrated consistently?”

---

## **IDP — Individual Development Plan**

### _How gaps are closed_

**IDP Focus**

- Behavior-based goals
- Real development actions
- Ongoing feedback
- Observable progress

**IDP Answers One Question**

> “Are leadership gaps being intentionally and effectively addressed?”

---

## **LDP — Leadership Development Pathway**

### _How readiness is validated_

**LDP Includes**

- Acting leadership assignments
- Expanded ownership
- Real decision-making
- Coaching without dependency

**LDP Answers One Question**

> “Can this leader execute independently at the next level?”

---

## **Promotion Readiness**

### _How decisions are made_

Promotion is considered when:

- Leadership behaviors are consistent
- Development gaps are closed
- Execution is validated
- Values are demonstrated under pressure

**Promotion Is**

- Evidence-based
- Values-aligned
- Supported by leaders

**Promotion Is Not**

- Automatic
- Time-based
- Guaranteed

---

## **Leader & Associate Roles**

### **Associate Owns**

- Daily leadership behavior
- Growth mindset
- Applying feedback
- Living CarMax values

### **Leader Owns**

- Coaching and feedback
- Development opportunities
- Objective assessment
- Promotion integrity

Leadership development is a **partnership**.

This protects:

- The associate
- The leader
- The team
- The business
