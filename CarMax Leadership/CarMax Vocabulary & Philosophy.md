# CarMax Vocabulary & Philosophy Playbook
**(Official Source of Truth)**

To maintain a unified and professional communication tone aligned with CarMax leadership standards across all training, recognition, and documentation messages. This guide provides reference phrases, vocabulary tone, and structure consistent with CarMax’s culture and values.

---

- **Our Vision:** To ensure an **iconic experience** for everyone, everywhere. An iconic experience means going above and beyond for our customers, our associates, and our communities.
- **Our Identity:** We are Team CarMax — customer-first, people-driven, integrity-led, innovation-powered, and excellence-focused.
- **Our Foundation:** CarMax is built around trust, control, and confidence.

## 2. Our Core Values in Action

- **We are proud members of Team CarMax.**
- **We execute for excellence in all we do.**
- **We strengthen the team by embracing our differences.**

- **We invest in our associates and provide opportunities to grow.**

### Go For Greatness
We don't settle — we strive to be the best.
- **We are industry disruptors, then & now.**
- **We relentlessly improve & innovate.**
- **We drive what’s possible.**

### Do The Right Thing
Integrity is non-negotiable.
- **We value integrity above all else.**
- **We treat everyone with respect.**
- **We strive to deliver the best product and experience.**
- Operating with honesty, ethics, and accountability.
- Doing what's right, even when it's not easy.
- **Language:** Emphasize integrity, accuracy, and ownership.

## 3. CarMax Culture
CarMax culture is defined by our commitment to integrity, mutual respect, and professional behavior. It is the foundation of Team CarMax and guides how we work together and represent our brand to partners and the public.

- **Integrity First:** Operating with absolute transparency, honesty, and accountability. Doing the right thing even when it is not easy.
- **Respectful Workplace:** Creating an environment free of disrespect, gossip, or inappropriate behavior. We treat all associates, leaders, and partners with dignity and professional courtesy.
- **Professional Boundaries:** Maintaining clear, professional distinctions in all supervisor-associate and peer-to-peer relationships, ensuring work environments remain comfortable, safe, and focused.
- **Brand Representation:** Understanding that when we are in the field, at dealership partners, or with customers, our language, volume, actions, and appearance represent the entire CarMax team. We communicate professionally at all times.
- **Supportive Leadership:** Feedback and correction are handled with empathy, constructive focus, and active listening, prioritizing learning and development rather than blame.
- **Values-Driven Culture:** Fostering a workplace centered on integrity, transparency, and putting people first, where teams are friendly, approachable, and invested in each other's success.
- **Straightforward Experience:** Delivering a fair, honest, and straightforward experience built on trust (such as the no-haggle pricing model).
  - **No-Haggle / Fair Pricing:** Clear, honest pricing customers can trust — no pressure, no games.
  - **Transparency:** Open communication, clear vehicle reports, and clear policies — no surprises.
- **Diversity, Community & Sustainability:**
  - **Diversity & Inclusion:** Different voices make us stronger. We welcome everyone, treat all with respect, and support belonging.
  - **Sustainability & Community:** We give back so communities thrive. We invest in ethical practices, our future, and environmental responsibility.
- **Learn & Grow (Associate Development):** We prioritize career growth and learning through a structured framework:
  - **Coaching:** Support starts on day one. You’ll be welcomed by leaders and peers who are ready to support your career journey through onboarding mentors, regular check-ins, and a culture that values connection and coaching.
  - **Development Programs:** Whether this is your first job, you’re looking for a new role, or want to grow your leadership skills, we offer development programs that help you build experience, expand your network, and grow your career with purpose.
  - **Skill Building:** We support your growth with real-world learning experiences, on-the-job training, and access to tools that help you build new skills, take on new challenges, and shape a career that fits your goals.

## 4. Communication Philosophy & Tone
CarMax managers communicate with a balance of **clarity, respect, and empathy.**

- **Tone:** Calm • Clear • Positive • Constructive
- **Goals:**
  - Reinforce teamwork and inclusion.
  - Promote learning and continuous improvement.
  - Reflect accountability without negativity.
  - Inspire confidence and professionalism.

## 6. Managers' Writing Toolkit

### The 5-Step Structure
1.  **Greeting:** Friendly and professional (e.g., “Good morning team,” or “Hi [Name],”).
2.  **Purpose Statement:** Clarify the "why" (e.g., “This is part of our Delivery Readiness series.”).
3.  **Key Points:** 3–5 bullet points focusing on clear behaviors.
4.  **Value Connection:** Reinforce the value (e.g., “Doing the Right Thing ensures customer trust.”).
5.  **Positive Close:** Express confidence (e.g., “Thanks for representing our team with professionalism.”).

### Message Tone Calibration
Before sending, ask:
*   **Is it clear?** Use direct language without jargon.
*   **Is it respectful?** Reflect understanding, not judgment.
*   **Is it motivating?** Reinforce purpose and pride.
*   **Is it aligned?** Connect to at least one CarMax value.
