### Objective & IDP Draft

**Objective**  
Strengthen team knowledge and accountability in mechanical awareness by incorporating real-world failure examples into training communications. Gustavo will publish at least 6 illustrated training messages to the Home Delivery team through the Team Text Group, using annotated photos to reinforce the importance of thorough pre-trip inspections.

**Description**  
Gustavo will demonstrate Results Focus and Safety by turning actual mechanical failures into shared learning opportunities for the team. For example, he published an annotated photo of an **Engine Fan Failure**, highlighting critical components such as the fan clutch, water pump, and fan blades. The message explained the risks of undetected damage and emphasized proactive inspection habits.

This approach reinforces the value of consistent under-hood checks by showing how early detection can prevent breakdowns, roadside delays, and costly repairs. By sharing these real-world examples, Gustavo promotes accountability, strengthens inspection routines, and equips team members with the awareness needed to identify potential issues before they become hazards.

📸 **Engine Fan Failure Example – September 14, 2025**  
I shared an annotated photo of a fan failure on one of our trucks with the Team Text Group. The message highlighted:

- The importance of visually inspecting fan blades during pre-trips.
- Checking for looseness in the fan clutch by gently shaking the assembly.
- Understanding that some failures are not noisy or visible at idle but can cause serious issues at highway speeds.

This real case reinforced why proactive under-hood inspections matter and how small checks prevent breakdowns. By converting this situation into a training tool, I helped the team grow their inspection awareness and continue our commitment to safety and Doing the Right Thing.
