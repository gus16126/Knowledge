CarMax Department Specific Self-Evaluation for June 2022.

June 2022.

After being an over-the-road owner-operator for several years, I began employment with CarMax in June 2022.

After my initial training period was done, I was asked by my manager at the time, Jessy Pesantes, to participate in a fleet safety program that required me to travel to Tampa, Florida, to attend a four-day training with the safety manager, Kyle Morgan.

As a member of the fleet team, and with a strong desire to contribute to its safety, I immediately shared with the lead and Sr. Fleet Drivers at my store several of the safety tips learned from my safety training with Kyle, emphasizing the importance of maintaining the upper deck eight feet or higher, and the lower extension and ramps, tucked in when empty to increase trailer visibility, and avoid accidents.
Gustavo Guallar
