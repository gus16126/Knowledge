# Gustavo Guallar 

A time you had to organize a plan for yourself or a team to reach a complex goal: 

## **Situation.** 

When I first started working at Pinkerton Security as a manager, I was made aware of the service quality issues that the company was facing. After examining the company’s operation, I determined that service quality issues were related to image and retention. 

## **Task.** 

My priority was to address the service quality as quickly as possible, which meant resolving the company's image and retention issues. 

## **Action.** 

I began visiting the clients individually to introduce myself and inquire about their concerns about the service. 

I arranged meetings with the security teams to discuss clients' concerns and their perspectives on possible solutions. 

I analyzed the schedules and made necessary changes to make them more efficient and beneficial for the security teams. 

I requested an appropriate budget for new uniforms to enhance the company's image. 

I requested a budget for radio and communication services for each account. 

Reviewed post orders for each account and made changes that reflected solutions to the client’s concerns. 

## **Result.** 

Overall service quality, along with employee retention and company image, increased considerably.
