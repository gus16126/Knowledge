
Championing our CarMax Values. Protects and preserves our CarMax Culture.

Answer:
After joining CarMax in June 2022, I was asked to participate in a fleet safety program,
which included a four-day training session in Tampa, Florida. As a member of the fleet
team, I was eager to contribute to our safety culture and immediately shared the key
safety tips I learned with the lead and senior fleet drivers at my store. I emphasized
practices such as maintaining the upper deck at eight feet or higher and ensuring the
lower extension and ramps were tucked in when empty to increase trailer visibility and
reduce the risk of accidents.
By taking the time to explain the reasoning behind these practices, I helped my team
understand their importance and encouraged their implementation. This proactive
approach not only reinforced a culture of safety but also demonstrated my commitment
to CarMax’s values:
• Put People First: I prioritized the safety and well-being of my team and the public
by sharing and implementing critical safety practices.
• Win Together: I collaborated with my team to ensure everyone understood and I
encouraged them to adopt these practices, fostering a shared commitment to
safety.
• Go for Greatness: I went beyond the training by actively applying what I learned
and striving to improve our safety standards.
• Do the Right Thing: I emphasized the importance of following safety protocols,
even when it required extra effort, because it was the right thing to do for our team
and customers.
This experience highlighted how living CarMax’s values can create a safer, more efficient,
and more collaborative work environment.
