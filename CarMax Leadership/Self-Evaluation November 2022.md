CarMax Department Specific Self-Evaluation for November 2022.

November 2022.

After identifying a need for safety improvements in the four-point wheel strapping method used on the rollback truck, I drew from my years of experience as a rollback driver to offer a safer load tie-down procedure.

To enhance my approach, I researched articles written by industry experts on this subject.

Following my research, I submitted an email to my lead driver, Luis Rejon, where I provided references and suggested safer operational procedures for the rollback four-point wheel strapping method.
