## **Purpose of the Master Leadership Binder**

The **CarMax Leadership Master Binder** consolidates Files **#1–#6** into a **single, authoritative leadership system** used to:

- Develop leaders consistently
- Assess readiness objectively
- Support fair, evidence-based promotion decisions
- Ensure alignment to CarMax Values at every stage

This binder is designed for **leaders, talent partners, and promotion decision forums** and reflects CarMax’s commitment to **developing leaders the right way**.

---

## **What This Binder Represents**

This binder is not a collection of documents.  
It is a **leadership operating system**.

It ensures that:

- Leadership expectations are clear
- Development is intentional
- Execution is validated
- Promotions are earned and defensible
