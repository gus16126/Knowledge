## **A time you helped solve a complicated customer or business problem:** 

## **Situation.** 

During my time as a manager at Pinkerton Security, I inherited an account from the prior manager, where the property manager for a high-profile client had decided to take charge of the security team and prohibited any oversight from Pinkerton’s management. 

Pinkerton’s manager and all field supervisors who attempted to exercise control over the security operations had been banned from the property. 

The security team assigned to the client has gone rogue and was not responding to Pinkertons’ management. 

This situation stemmed from the client’s dissatisfaction with the level of service provided and management’s response to the client’s concerns. 

## **Task.** 

Headquarters requested that I prioritize regaining complete control of security operations for this account immediately, or they will be compelled to take legal action against the client for breach of contract. 

## **Action.** 

I tried to communicate with the team assigned to the property and the client’s property manager to gain information about the cause of the situation, but I did not achieve any positive results. 

I found the business location of the community association president and visited him.

Introduced myself as the new manager and provided an overview of the current situation. I detailed my failed attempts to contact their property manager and the hostile reactions to my efforts at supervising the security team. 

I also identified the breaches in the contractual agreements and the legal liabilities facing the community and subsequently requested a meeting with the community board members to address these concerns. 

## **Result.** 

1. A direct line of communication was established with the community board of directors to allow them to communicate their future service concerns directly to me. 

2. A new security team loyal to Pinkerton’s management, trained by the field supervisors and me, assumed responsibility for the security operations at the property. 

3. The property manager was instructed not to interfere with Pinkerton's security operations at the property.
