# **CarMax Leadership Promotion Deck**

## **Executive Presentation Framework**

### **MAP · IDP · LDP → Promotion Decision**

This is the **promotion decision deck** leaders can use in calibration forums, talent reviews, and succession discussions. It translates the Master Leadership Binder into a **clear, executive-ready story**—concise, evidence-based, and values-aligned.

---

## **Slide 1 — Title & Purpose**

**CarMax Leadership Promotion Review**

**Purpose**

- Present leadership readiness clearly and consistently
- Support fair, evidence-based promotion decisions
- Align leaders around CarMax Values and standards

---

## **Slide 2 — CarMax Leadership Philosophy**

**How CarMax Develops Leaders**

- Leaders are developed, not selected by tenure
- Readiness is demonstrated through behavior
- Promotions are earned through consistency and impact
- Values guide decisions at every stage

**Values in Action**

- Win Together
- Put People First
- Go for Greatness
- Do the Right Thing

---

## **Slide 3 — Leadership Pipeline Overview**

**End-to-End Readiness Model**

**MAP → IDP → LDP → Promotion Decision**

- MAP assesses readiness
- IDP closes development gaps
- LDP validates execution
- Promotion confirms sustained leadership capability

---

- MAP Readiness Status
    - Ready Now / Developing / Not Yet Ready
