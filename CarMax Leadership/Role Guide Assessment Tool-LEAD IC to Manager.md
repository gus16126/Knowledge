# Role Guide Assessment Tool: LEAD / IC to Manager 

The Role Guide Assessment Tool is a CarMax internal performance and calibration document designed to evaluate high-performing Leads and Individual Contributors (ICs) who are transitioning toward management roles.

The tool provides a framework for managers to assess an associate's current performance against the expectations of their current level and the "Next Level" (Manager).

**Core Purpose and Best Practices**
The primary goal is to calibrate performance at regular intervals to ensure consistency in talent development. Key best practices for managers using this tool include:

**Specific Examples:** Managers are instructed to type specific examples for each "Role Guide Area".

**Collaboration:** The tool should be reviewed with the associate during check-ins and updated when calibrating with other leaders.

**Critical Reflection:** Managers must ask themselves if the associate’s behavior is a Recurrence vs. Occurrence, if it was Initiated vs. Instructed, and if the observations are Validated by others

**Questions to askyourself:**

**Recurrence vs. Occurrence.** What should they demonstrate? How often have they demonstrated this? 
**Initiated vs. Instructed.** Did they proactively recognize and take advantage of the opportunity? Did they demonstrate at the Next Level without prompting? 
**Validated vs. Viewpoint.** How should I observe this? Who else can provide feedback/observations on this?
