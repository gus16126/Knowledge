**Purpose of the CarMax Leadership Development Program**

CarMax created its leadership development programs to build a strong pipeline of capable leaders who can support associates, drive operational excellence, and uphold the company's values and culture. The program is designed to help associates develop the skills, knowledge, and behaviors needed to lead effectively while promoting teamwork, safety, accountability, and continuous improvement.

By investing in leadership development, CarMax demonstrates its commitment to Put People First by creating opportunities for associates to grow professionally and advance their careers. The program also supports the company's long-term success by preparing future leaders who can help teams Win Together, Go for Greatness, and Do the Right Thing while delivering exceptional experiences for customers and communities.
Gustavo Guallar
